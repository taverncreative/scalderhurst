Product Range

Our range is built around carton and packaging applications, with folding boxboard at the centre. Every product is available in reel or sheet form, and our in-house conversion facility can cut, slit, rewind or rewrap to your required dimensions. All grades are available with FSC or PEFC certification on request.

Folding Boxboard (GC1 & GC2)

Our folding boxboard range covers the full spectrum of carton applications, with food-grade GC1 and GC2 held in stock at our Kent warehouse. These coated grades are the standard choice for carton manufacture where print quality, whiteness and food-contact compliance matter.

White-Lined Chipboard

A recycled-fibre carton board with a white printable top liner, widely used for everyday packaging where a strong visual finish is needed without the cost of a virgin grade.

Solid Board

Rigid boards in a full range of thicknesses for set-up boxes, luxury packaging, greetings cards and premium carton applications where stiffness and a substantial feel are important.

Speciality Grades

Beyond the core range, we supply metallised, embossed, coated and food-contact-approved speciality boards for customers whose work needs a particular finish, barrier property or tactile effect.

Industries We Supply

Our board range is specified across every sector where carton packaging matters. We work closely with converters and brand owners in the industries below, matching grade and format to the job in hand.

Food & Beverage

Food-grade GC1 and GC2 for cartons, sleeves and retail-ready packaging. All food-contact grades are fully traceable and meet current UK food-contact requirements, supporting manufacturers, processors and co-packers across the sector.

Pharmaceuticals

Consistent, high-quality folding boxboard for medicine cartons, patient information leaflets and secondary packaging. Reliable supply and consistent print performance are essential in an industry where every carton has to meet strict quality and regulatory standards.

Cosmetics

Premium coated boards and speciality finishes for cosmetic and personal care cartons, where brand presentation is everything. We supply the grades that allow converters to deliver the colour, surface and tactile quality the sector expects.

Greetings Cards

Solid and coated boards for greetings cards, postcards and invitations. We support both high-volume commercial publishers and smaller independent card makers, with grades chosen for stiffness, print quality and a quality feel in the hand.

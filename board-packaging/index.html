Product Range

Our board and packaging range covers the grades most widely used in the corrugated, carton and display industries. Every product is available in reel or sheet form, and our in-house conversion facility can cut, slit or rewind to your required dimensions. All grades are available with FSC or PEFC certification on request.

Corrugated Case Materials

We stock the full spectrum of papers used in corrugated board manufacture. Our range includes semi-chemical and recycled fluting mediums from 90 gsm to 175 gsm, testliner grades from 115 gsm to 300 gsm in both brown and white-top finishes, and virgin kraftliner from 125 gsm to 400 gsm. These materials are supplied in reels sized to suit all standard corrugator widths.

Folding Boxboard

Our folding boxboard selection covers single-ply and multi-ply grades from 200 gsm to 500 gsm, suitable for carton production, pharmaceutical packaging, food contact applications and premium retail boxes. We supply both GC (coated) and GN (uncoated) grades, including SBS (solid bleached sulphate) board for applications where print quality and whiteness are critical.

Solid Board

For applications requiring rigidity and stacking strength, we supply grey chipboard, white-lined chipboard and solid fibreboard in thicknesses from 0.5 mm to 3.0 mm. These grades are widely used in book covers, luxury packaging, set-up boxes and point-of-sale displays where structural integrity matters.

Liner & Fluting Grades

Beyond standard corrugated case materials, we hold a dedicated range of performance liners and fluting grades engineered for specific end uses. This includes high-performance recycled testliners with enhanced burst and crush resistance, lightweight flutings that reduce material usage without sacrificing strength, and wet-strength liners designed for chilled and frozen supply chains.

Speciality Papers

Our speciality paper range includes food-contact-approved grease-resistant papers, PE-coated board for liquid packaging, kraft wrapping papers from 40 gsm to 120 gsm, and silicone-coated release liners. We also supply interleaving papers, tissue paper for protective wrapping, and waxed papers for moisture-sensitive applications.

Applications

Food & Beverage Packaging

From corrugated transit cases for bottled goods to folding boxboard cartons for cereals, confectionery and frozen foods, our board grades meet the demands of the food and beverage sector. We supply food-contact-compliant materials tested to BfR and EU Regulation 1935/2004 standards, giving manufacturers and co-packers confidence that their packaging is safe, consistent and fully traceable.

Retail & Point of Sale

High-quality folding boxboard and solid board are essential for shelf-ready packaging, counter display units and branded retail cartons. Our white-top and coated grades deliver excellent printability for litho, flexo and digital processes, helping brands achieve the visual impact needed to stand out on the shelf. We supply board for everything from cosmetics packaging to electronics retail boxes.

Transit & Protective Packaging

Heavy-duty kraftliner and high-performance fluting grades form the backbone of transit packaging that protects goods throughout the supply chain. Whether you manufacture single-wall cases for e-commerce fulfilment or triple-wall containers for automotive parts, we stock the liner and fluting combinations you need. Our technical team can advise on the right material specification to balance protection with cost efficiency.

Warehousing & Logistics

From our Kent-based warehouses we store, manage and distribute paper and board products across the UK and Ireland. With capacity for 7,500 to 10,000 tonnes of stockholding, we keep your supply chain moving with reliable inventory management and scheduled deliveries tailored to your production requirements.

Key Benefits

Why businesses across the UK and Ireland trust Scalderhurst with their paper and board logistics.

Large-Scale Capacity

Our warehouses hold between 7,500 and 10,000 tonnes of paper and board at any given time. This substantial stockholding means we can absorb demand fluctuations and fulfil orders rapidly, even during peak periods or when mill lead times extend.

Flexible Delivery Options

Whether you need regular scheduled deliveries to support a just-in-time production line or ad-hoc consignments at short notice, we tailor our distribution to suit your operations. Full and part loads are available with tracked vehicles across mainland UK and Ireland.

Inventory Management

We manage your stock levels proactively so you never face an unexpected shortage. Our team monitors consumption patterns, manages reorder points and coordinates mill replenishment to keep your supply chain uninterrupted throughout the year.

Secure, Controlled Storage

Paper and board require careful handling. Our warehouses maintain dry, clean and temperature-stable conditions to preserve product quality from intake to dispatch. Full traceability through FSC and PEFC chain of custody ensures every pallet is accounted for.

Warehouse Capabilities

A closer look at the infrastructure and services that underpin our warehousing operation.

Storage Facilities

Our purpose-built warehousing in Kent provides dedicated racking and block-stacking for reels, pallets and sheets of every grade. The facilities are designed specifically for paper and board storage, with concrete floors, covered loading bays and controlled ventilation to prevent moisture damage. Stock is organised by grade, customer and delivery schedule, enabling rapid picking and efficient space utilisation across the full 7,500 to 10,000 tonne capacity.

Inventory Management

Every item entering our warehouse is logged, tracked and monitored through our stock management system. We provide customers with regular stock reports, manage minimum and maximum holding levels, and coordinate replenishment orders with European mills on your behalf. This hands-off approach means you can focus on production while we ensure the right grades are always available in the right quantities. Over 50 years of experience in the paper trade means we understand seasonal demand cycles and can plan stockholding accordingly.

Distribution Network

From our Kent base we operate a distribution network covering the entire UK mainland and Ireland. Deliveries are planned and routed for efficiency, with the flexibility to handle both full articulated loads and smaller part-load consignments. Our logistics team works closely with haulage partners to maintain a dependable schedule, and every vehicle is tracked so you know exactly when your delivery will arrive. For customers with multiple sites, we coordinate split deliveries to ensure each location receives exactly what it needs.

Order Fulfilment

When you place an order, our warehouse team picks, checks and dispatches your goods with precision. Whether the requirement is for converted sheets cut to specification, full reels direct from stock or a mixed consignment combining several grades, we handle the preparation and loading to get it right first time. Orders placed before our daily cut-off are typically dispatched same day or next day, depending on destination and load requirements.

Delivery Coverage

Reliable distribution wherever you need it, from single-site manufacturers to multi-location operations.

UK Mainland

We deliver to all regions of mainland England, Scotland and Wales. Standard lead times are typically one to three working days depending on distance and load size, with next-day service available for many south-east and midlands destinations. Regular delivery runs to key industrial areas mean we can offer competitive rates and consistent schedules for customers with ongoing supply requirements.

Ireland

Scalderhurst has long-established logistics routes to both Northern Ireland and the Republic of Ireland. We manage the cross-channel logistics so you receive a seamless service, with consolidated loads shipped weekly to keep costs efficient and supply dependable for our Irish customers.

Scheduled & Ad-Hoc Deliveries

For customers with predictable consumption, we arrange regular scheduled deliveries aligned to your production calendar. This call-off approach reduces your on-site storage burden and ensures stock arrives precisely when needed. For urgent or one-off requirements, our ad-hoc delivery service can respond quickly with part-load or express consignments to keep your lines running.

Tracked Consignments

Every delivery leaving our warehouse is tracked from dispatch to arrival. Our logistics team provides proof of delivery and can give you real-time updates on consignment status. For time-critical deliveries, we offer direct communication with the driver to coordinate arrival windows and ensure smooth goods-in at your premises.

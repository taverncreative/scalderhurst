Paper & Board for Printing & Publishing

Scalderhurst supplies coated and uncoated printing papers, cover boards and speciality substrates to commercial printers, book publishers and packaging converters across the UK. With up to 10,000 tonnes in stock and in-house sheeting, we deliver the right paper in the right format, fast.

Our Printing & Publishing Range

We stock a broad selection of grades from leading European mills, held at our Kent warehouse for next-day dispatch. Our in-house conversion service means we can sheet reels to any size your presses require.

Printing Papers

Woodfree offset, bulky bookworm, machine-finished and super-calendered grades for litho, digital and web-offset printing. Available in grammages from 60gsm to 170gsm in both long-grain and short-grain orientations.

Cover Boards

Coated and uncoated cover boards from 170gsm to 400gsm for book covers, brochures, menus, folders and premium marketing collateral. We carry white, ivory and tinted options from mills known for consistency and printability.

Speciality Substrates

Textured, embossed, laid and recycled papers for invitations, annual reports, luxury packaging and creative print projects. We also supply synthetic and tear-resistant substrates for labels and outdoor applications.

Coated Papers

Gloss, silk and matt coated papers for high-quality colour reproduction in catalogues, magazines, brochures and promotional print. Available in sheets or reels with consistent batch-to-batch quality.

Uncoated Papers

Premium uncoated offset and natural-finish papers for book text blocks, stationery, letterheads and direct mail. These grades offer excellent readability, good opacity and reliable runnability on all press types.

Applications

Our papers and boards serve printers and publishers working across a wide range of end uses.

Commercial Print

Brochures, catalogues, leaflets, posters, direct mail and marketing materials. We supply the grades most commonly specified by designers and print buyers, with the flexibility to source unusual weights and finishes on request.

Book Publishing

Text papers, cover boards and endpaper stock for trade books, educational titles, journals and coffee-table editions. We understand the specific requirements of book printers, including bulk, opacity and grain direction.

Packaging Print

Folding boxboard, white-lined chipboard and SBS board for printed packaging, cartons, sleeves and labels. We supply converters who print and die-cut packaging for food, cosmetics, pharmaceuticals and consumer goods.

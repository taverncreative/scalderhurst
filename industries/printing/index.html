Paper & Board for Printing & Publishing

Scalderhurst supplies coated and uncoated printing papers, cover boards and speciality substrates to commercial printers, book publishers and packaging converters across the UK. With up to 9,500 tonnes in stock and in-house sheeting, we deliver the right paper in the right format, fast.

Our Paper Range

Scalderhurst supplies a full range of graphical papers sourced from leading mills worldwide. We hold stock at our Kent warehouse for rapid dispatch, and our in-house conversion service lets us sheet or rewind to the exact size and format your presses need.

Woodfree Coated

High-quality coated papers with a smooth, receptive surface for sharp image reproduction and accurate colour. Available in gloss, silk and matt finishes across a full range of weights.

Applications include:

  • Magazines
  • Brochures
  • Leaflets
  • Flyers
  • Catalogues and annual reports

Woodfree Uncoated

Premium uncoated grades with a natural feel, strong opacity and reliable runnability on all press types. A go-to choice where readability and a tactile finish matter.

Applications include:

  • Business documents
  • Letterheads and stationery
  • Book text blocks
  • Direct mail
  • Brochures and reports

Lightweight Coated (LWC)

Coated mechanical papers designed for high-volume, cost-efficient print runs. LWC offers good print quality and bulk at lower weights, making it ideal for large-format and high-page-count work.

Applications include:

  • Magazines
  • Catalogues
  • Supplements and inserts
  • Leaflets and flyers

Mediumweight Coated (MWC)

A step up from LWC, with improved bulk, opacity and surface quality. MWC suits work that needs a more substantial feel without moving to a full woodfree coated grade.

Applications include:

  • Premium magazines
  • Brochures
  • Catalogues
  • Promotional leaflets

Copier and Business Papers

Office grades engineered for consistent performance on digital presses, copiers and office printers. Smooth running, clean feed and reliable output across long and short runs.

Applications include:

  • Business documents
  • Reports and proposals
  • Letterheads
  • Digital print runs

Envelope Papers

Dedicated envelope grades in a range of finishes, weights and shades, supplied in reel or sheet form to suit converting and print-and-fulfil operations.

Applications include:

  • Business correspondence
  • Direct mail
  • Transactional envelopes
  • Branded and promotional mailings

Applications

Our papers and boards serve printers and publishers working across a wide range of end uses.

Commercial Print

Brochures, catalogues, leaflets, posters, direct mail and marketing materials. We supply the grades most commonly specified by designers and print buyers, with the flexibility to source unusual weights and finishes on request.

Book Publishing

Text papers, cover boards and endpaper stock for trade books, educational titles, journals and coffee-table editions. We understand the specific requirements of book printers, including bulk, opacity and grain direction.

Packaging Print

Folding boxboard, white-lined chipboard and SBS board for printed packaging, cartons, sleeves and labels. We supply converters who print and die-cut packaging for food, cosmetics, pharmaceuticals and consumer goods.

Looking for a Reliable Paper Supplier?

Whether you need a pallet of coated art or a full lorry-load of offset, our team can quote and deliver quickly. We hold extensive stock in Kent and offer in-house sheeting to your exact press size.

Industries We Serve

Scalderhurst supplies paper, board and packaging grades to carton manufacturers, brand owners and converters across the sectors where quality, reliability and speed matter most. From greeting-card publishers to pharmaceutical packaging specialists, we match grade, format and lead time to each industry's needs.

Our Sectors

We work closely with manufacturers, converters and brand owners across five core sectors, with the grades, conversion services and supply-chain support each one depends on.

Carton Manufacturers & Packaging

Folding boxboard, solid board and speciality grades supplied to carton manufacturers producing retail-ready cartons, sleeves and rigid packaging across every consumer sector.

Greeting Cards

Solid and coated boards for greeting-card publishers, postcard producers and independent card makers. Grades selected for stiffness, whiteness and a quality feel in the hand.

Cosmetics

Premium coated boards and speciality finishes for cosmetic, fragrance and personal-care cartons where brand presentation, tactile finish and colour performance matter most.

Pharmaceuticals

Consistent, traceable folding boxboard for medicine cartons, patient information leaflets and secondary packaging — supplied to the quality and regulatory standards the sector demands.

Food & Beverage

Food-grade GC1 and GC2 for cartons, sleeves and retail-ready packaging. Fully traceable food-contact grades meeting current UK and EU requirements.

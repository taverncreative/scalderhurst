Industries We Serve

Scalderhurst supplies paper, board and packaging grades to carton manufacturers, brand owners and converters across the sectors where quality, reliability and speed matter most. From greeting-card publishers to pharmaceutical packaging specialists, we match grade, format and lead time to each industry's needs.

Our Sectors

We work closely with manufacturers, converters and brand owners across three core sectors, supplying the grades, conversion services and supply-chain support each one depends on.

Carton Manufacturing

Folding boxboard, solid board and speciality packaging grades for carton converters serving greeting cards, cosmetics, pharmaceuticals and food & beverage brands.

Learn more about Carton Manufacturing

Printing & Publishing

Coated and uncoated graphical papers, cover boards and speciality substrates for commercial printers, publishers and packaging converters.

Learn more about Printing & Publishing

Retail & E-Commerce

Mailers, point-of-sale display boards and transit packaging for online retailers, fulfilment houses and high-street brands.

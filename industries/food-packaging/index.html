Paper & Board for Food Packaging

Scalderhurst supplies food-contact approved paper and board to processors, manufacturers and retailers across the United Kingdom. Every product we hold for the food sector meets current UK food-contact regulations, backed by full traceability from mill to dispatch.

Products for the Food Industry

We stock a comprehensive range of paper and board grades designed specifically for food packaging applications. All materials are sourced from FSC and PEFC certified mills and held at our Kent warehouse for rapid dispatch.

Food-Grade Kraft & Wrapping Papers

Unbleached and bleached kraft papers for wrapping bakery goods, fresh produce liners and general food-contact use. Available in a full range of weights and in reel or sheet form to suit automated packing lines.

Folding Boxboard for Retail-Ready Packaging

Premium GC1 and GC2 folding boxboard sourced from leading mills worldwide, ideal for retail-ready packaging, cereal cartons, confectionery boxes and chilled food sleeves. Available in a full range of weights.

Grease-Resistant Liners

Greaseproof and grease-resistant papers for bakery trays, confectionery wrapping, takeaway containers and oven-ready meal packaging. These liners prevent oil and fat migration while maintaining food safety compliance.

Food-Contact Interleaving Papers

Lightweight interleaving sheets for separating sliced meats, cheese, fish fillets and bakery products during processing and retail display. All grades are food-contact approved and available in custom cut sizes through our in-house conversion service.

Shelf-Ready Packaging Trays

SRP trays and display-ready cases designed to move directly from pallet to supermarket shelf. We can sheet or slit to the exact blank size your converter requires.

Compliance & Food Safety

Food safety is non-negotiable. Scalderhurst maintains rigorous standards across every stage of our supply chain to ensure the materials we deliver are safe, traceable and fully compliant.

Food-Contact Regulations

All food-contact materials comply with Regulation (EC) No 1935/2004 and relevant UK retained legislation. We hold declarations of compliance and certificates of conformity for every food-grade product in our range.

BRC Storage & Distribution

Our Kent warehouse operates in line with BRC Global Standard for Storage and Distribution requirements, ensuring hygienic handling, pest management and contamination prevention throughout the storage and dispatch process.

Full Traceability

Every delivery is traceable back to the producing mill, with batch numbers, production dates and chain-of-custody documentation available on request. Our FSC and PEFC certifications provide additional assurance of responsible sourcing.

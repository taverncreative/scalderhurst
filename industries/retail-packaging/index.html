Packaging for Retail & E-Commerce

Scalderhurst supplies corrugated mailers, display boards and point-of-sale materials to online retailers, fulfilment houses and high-street brands across the United Kingdom. We hold extensive stock at our Kent warehouse and offer in-house conversion for bespoke sizes and formats.

Products for Retail & E-Commerce

The growth of online retail has transformed packaging requirements. We supply the materials that protect products in transit, present brands in-store and help businesses manage fulfilment efficiently.

Corrugated Mailers

Single-wall and double-wall corrugated mailer blanks for e-commerce fulfilment in a range of standard and bespoke sizes. Our mailer-grade boards are designed for reliable performance on automated packing lines and provide excellent crush resistance during transit.

Display Boards

White-lined and kraft-lined corrugated boards for free-standing display units, shelf-edge displays and counter-top stands. We supply flat sheets and pre-scored blanks that converters can print, laminate and die-cut for retail environments.

Point-of-Sale Materials

Solid board, mount board and foam board substrates for POS displays, hanging signs, shelf wobblers and promotional headers. Available in white, coloured and recycled options with excellent surface quality for digital and litho print.

E-Commerce Packaging

Void-fill papers, wrapping tissues, cushioning pads and paper-based alternatives to plastic packaging materials. As retailers move toward sustainable fulfilment, we supply the FSC and PEFC certified paper products that replace bubble wrap, polystyrene chips and plastic void-fill.

Transit & Protective Packaging

Heavy-duty corrugated sheets, edge protectors and layer pads for palletised goods, fragile items and high-value products. We can sheet and slit to exact dimensions through our in-house conversion facility, reducing waste and improving packing efficiency.

Why Retailers Choose Scalderhurst

Stock Availability

With up to 10,000 tonnes of paper and board held in our Kent warehouse, we can fulfil orders at short notice. Most standard grades are available for next-day delivery anywhere in the UK.

Sustainable Materials

Every product in our retail range is sourced from responsibly managed forests. Our FSC and PEFC chain-of-custody certifications give your brand the environmental credentials your customers expect.

Conversion to Size

Our in-house sheeting and slitting service means you receive board cut to your exact blank size, eliminating off-cuts on your production floor and speeding up your packing process.

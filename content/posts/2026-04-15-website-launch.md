<p>Welcome to the new Scalderhurst website. We've rebuilt the whole thing from the ground up, with clearer information on what we supply, what we can convert, and how our warehousing and logistics work.</p>
<h2>What's new</h2>
<p>The new site makes it easier to:</p>
<ul>
<li>See our full range of <strong>folding boxboard, carton and speciality packaging grades</strong>, including food-grade GC1 and GC2 held in stock</li>
<li>Understand our <strong>in-house conversion capabilities</strong> — sheeting, slitting, rewinding, guillotining, rewrapping, export packing and pallet turning</li>
<li>Find out about our <strong>warehousing and UK distribution</strong> — up to 9,500 tonnes in stock, high-roof storage, and a flexible home for imported goods and other non-paper stock</li>
<li>Read <strong>lorry-friendly directions</strong> to Ford Mill and Pivington Mill, with the Harrietsham bridge height restriction flagged up front</li>
</ul>
<h2>What's next</h2>
<p>We'll be posting regularly here — company updates, industry insights, and anything we've got in stock that might be of interest. If you'd like to be kept in the loop, <a href="/contact/">get in touch</a> and we'll add you to the list.</p>
<p>Any feedback on the site is welcome. Thanks for stopping by.</p>

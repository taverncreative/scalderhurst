Export

Scalderhurst sources and supplies paper and board to customers worldwide. With over 50 years of experience and established relationships with mills across the globe, we handle international orders with the same reliability our UK customers depend on.

What We Offer

Global Sourcing

We source paper and board from mills worldwide, giving us access to a wide range of grades, weights and specifications. If you need material that isn't readily available in your local market, we can find it and get it to you.

International Order Handling

We have experience managing export orders from initial enquiry through to delivery. Our team handles documentation, coordinates shipping logistics and ensures material is packed and prepared to the standards international transit requires.

Export Packing

All export orders are packed at our Kent facility to withstand long-distance transit. This includes secure wrapping, palletising, labelling and load preparation — so goods arrive at their destination in the condition they left ours.

Why Work With Us

Established Supply Network

Over 50 years of trading has given us direct relationships with mills across the globe. We know where to source what you need, and we can move quickly when availability is tight.

Stock Availability

With up to 9,500 tonnes held at our Kent warehouse, we can often fulfil export orders from existing stock — reducing lead times compared with ordering direct from a mill.

In-House Conversion

Material can be sheeted, slit, rewound or repacked to your specification before it ships. This means you receive goods ready to use, not parent reels that need further processing at your end.

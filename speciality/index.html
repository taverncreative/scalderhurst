Speciality Product Range

Our speciality range covers grades and finishes that sit outside standard board and packaging categories. These products serve demanding applications across food, pharmaceutical, display, industrial and creative sectors.

Food-Contact Papers & Boards

Grease-resistant, moisture-barrier and direct food-contact papers certified to EU Regulation 1935/2004. Used for bakery interleaving, tray liners, wrapping papers and food-safe packaging inserts.

Coated & Laminated Boards

Clay-coated, PE-coated and foil-laminated boards for applications requiring enhanced printability, moisture resistance or barrier properties. Available in a range of weights and finishes.

Fire-Retardant Boards

Boards treated to meet fire retardancy standards for use in exhibition displays, retail environments, construction hoarding and interior fit-out projects where fire compliance is mandatory.

Technical & Industrial Papers

Interleaving papers, release papers, anti-tarnish papers and VCI (volatile corrosion inhibitor) papers for industrial and manufacturing applications. Used to protect surfaces, separate components and prevent corrosion during storage and transit.

Display & Creative Boards

High-white, coloured, textured and metallic boards for point-of-sale displays, exhibition stands, architectural models and creative applications. Available in a range of thicknesses and surface finishes.

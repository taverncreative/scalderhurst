Our Conversion Capabilities

Sheeting

We convert parent reels into flat sheets at our dedicated sheeting line. Reels are unwound under controlled tension, cut to your specified sheet length and stacked on pallets ready for dispatch. We handle a wide range of substrates including kraft liner, testliner, fluting, folding boxboard and speciality papers. Standard sheet widths up to 1,600 mm are available, with lengths cut to your exact requirement. Tolerances of +/- 1 mm on length and width are maintained as standard.

Slitting

Our slitting equipment converts wide parent reels into narrower rolls for use in corrugating, printing, wrapping and converting machinery. We slit reels from a maximum web width of 2,500 mm down to finished widths as narrow as 50 mm. Core diameters of 76 mm and 150 mm are stocked, with other sizes available on request. Finished reels are wrapped and labelled to your specification, ready to load straight onto your machine.

Guillotine Cutting

For precise trimming of sheets and smaller format work, our industrial guillotine handles board up to 1,800 mm in width. This service is suited to customers who require non-standard sheet sizes, trimmed blanks or sample batches. We regularly cut corrugated case material, solid board, greyboard and coated papers to bespoke dimensions for packaging prototypes, point-of-sale displays and specialist print applications.

Custom Specifications

Not every requirement fits a standard format. We work with customers to produce bespoke sizes, ream quantities and packing configurations that integrate directly into their production workflow. Whether you need interleaving sheets cut to match a specific tray size, board blanks bundled in exact counts or reels wound to a precise outer diameter, our team will configure the order to suit. Talk to your account manager about any non-standard requirement and we will confirm feasibility and lead time.

Quality Assurance

Quality is embedded at every stage of the conversion process. All incoming reels and sheets are checked against mill test certificates before processing begins. During conversion, our operators monitor dimensions, edge quality and surface condition continuously. Finished goods are inspected and recorded against your purchase order before palletising and wrapping.

Scalderhurst holds both FSC and PEFC chain-of-custody certification, providing independently verified traceability from the forest to your factory. Every delivery is accompanied by the relevant certification documentation, ensuring your supply chain meets responsible sourcing requirements for retail, food packaging and export markets.

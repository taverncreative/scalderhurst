Conversion Capabilities

Three sheeters in operation, plus the largest rewinder outside of a mill — giving us the scale and flexibility to handle anything from a single pallet to long-term contracted programmes.

Sheeting

Parent reels converted into flat sheets on our dedicated sheeters, stacked and palletised ready for dispatch.

  • Maximum sheet size: 1,650 × 2,200 mm
  • Custom sheet sizes to your specification
  • Sheet length range: 400 mm to 1,200 mm
  • Maximum sheets out per pass: 3

Sheeting Input Specification

  • Maximum reel width in: 1,420 mm (1,400 mm trimmed)
  • Maximum reel diameter in: 1,300 mm
  • Maximum reel weight: 2,000 kg
  • Core sizes accepted: 76 mm and 152 mm
  • Six-reel back-stand for fast reel changes

Rewinding

Parent reels rewound to new widths, diameters or core sizes on the largest rewinder outside of a mill — ideal for envelope manufacturers, narrow-web converters and press-ready supply.

  • Minimum rewound width: 150 mm
  • Maximum parent reel width: 3.3 m
  • Maximum outbound reel diameter: 1,500 mm
  • Wide range of core sizes available

Guillotining

Programmable Wohlenberg guillotines for precise trimming, size conversion and sample-batch work on stacked sheets and boards.

  • Maximum cut width: 1,550 mm
  • Wohlenberg programmable guillotines
  • Ideal for non-standard sheet sizes, trimmed blanks, point-of-sale and prototype runs

Sustainability Through Reuse and Reduced Waste

Conversion isn't just about making material fit — it's one of the most practical ways to reduce waste across a paper and board supply chain. Cutting to size on site means customers only receive what they can use, so less material ends up as offcut.

Reworking Redundant Stock

When stock is no longer needed for its original order, we rework it wherever we can — guillotining to new sizes, rewinding to different widths, or repackaging for a new customer. Material that would otherwise be written off gets a second life.

Converting to Size

By sheeting, slitting and cutting to your exact specification, we help customers avoid the offcuts and trim waste that come with buying standard sizes and trimming down in-house.

Reducing Waste Across the Operation

Careful handling, controlled storage and efficient planning keep damage, mis-cuts and scrap to a minimum. What waste we do generate is sorted and sent for recycling.

Quality Assurance

Quality is embedded at every stage of the conversion process. All incoming reels and sheets are checked against mill test certificates before processing begins. During conversion, our operators monitor dimensions, edge quality and surface condition continuously. Finished goods are inspected and recorded against your purchase order before palletising and wrapping.

Scalderhurst holds both FSC and PEFC chain-of-custody certification, providing independently verified traceability from the forest to your factory. Every delivery is accompanied by the relevant certification documentation, ensuring your supply chain meets responsible sourcing requirements for retail, food packaging and export markets.

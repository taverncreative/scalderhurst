About Scalderhurst

For over 50 years, Scalderhurst has been a trusted supplier of paper and board to converters, printers and manufacturers across the United Kingdom. Built on family values and deep industry knowledge, we deliver the products and service our customers depend on.

Our Story

Scalderhurst was founded in 1973 as a small independent paper merchant based in Kent. From the outset, the business was driven by a straightforward principle: understand what customers need and deliver it reliably, every time. That approach resonated with local converters and printers, and the company quickly earned a reputation for dependable supply and honest dealing.

Through the 1980s and 1990s, Scalderhurst expanded its product range and warehousing capacity to meet growing demand. We invested in dedicated storage facilities and built direct relationships with leading European paper and board mills. This allowed us to hold significant stock in the UK, reducing lead times and giving our customers the security of guaranteed availability even during periods of market volatility.

Today, Scalderhurst maintains between 7,500 and 10,000 tonnes of paper and board in stock at any given time. We supply businesses of all sizes across the UK and Ireland, from independent converters to large-scale packaging manufacturers. Our in-house conversion capabilities, including sheeting and slitting, mean we can deliver materials cut precisely to specification without the delays of third-party processing.

Despite more than five decades of growth, Scalderhurst remains a family business at heart. The values that guided us in 1973 still shape every decision we make: treat customers as partners, maintain the highest standards of quality and traceability, and always look for ways to do things better. That continuity of approach is what sets us apart in an industry where long-term reliability matters.

What We Do

Scalderhurst provides a complete supply chain for paper and board, from sourcing and stockholding through to conversion and nationwide delivery. Our services are designed to work together, giving you a single point of contact for all your material needs.

Board & Packaging

Corrugated case materials, folding boxboard, solid board, liner grades and kraft papers sourced from trusted European mills. We stock a comprehensive range of weights, grades and formats ready for prompt dispatch.

Conversion Services

In-house sheeting and slitting to your exact dimensions. Our conversion facility processes reels and sheets to precise tolerances, with quality checks at every stage and no minimum order requirements on standard grades.

Warehousing & Logistics

Secure, dedicated warehousing in Kent with capacity for thousands of tonnes. We manage scheduled deliveries, call-off orders and just-in-time supply across the UK and Ireland using our own vehicles and trusted haulage partners.

Speciality Products

Specialist papers and boards for niche applications including food-contact materials, grease-resistant papers, interleaving sheets and technical substrates for industrial use.

Our Values

Reliability

Our customers depend on consistent, on-time supply to keep their production lines running. We maintain substantial UK stockholding and strong mill relationships so that we can fulfil orders promptly, even when market conditions are challenging. When we commit to a delivery date, we deliver.

Quality

Every product that leaves our warehouse is fully traceable and meets the specifications agreed with our customers. We work exclusively with reputable mills and carry out rigorous quality checks throughout our supply chain, from goods-in inspection to final dispatch. Our customers know they can trust what we supply.

Partnership

We believe the best business relationships are built over years, not transactions. Every customer is assigned a dedicated account manager who understands their requirements, anticipates their needs and acts as a genuine extension of their procurement team. Many of our customer relationships span decades.

Sustainability

Responsible sourcing is fundamental to how we operate. We hold both FSC and PEFC chain-of-custody certification, ensuring that the paper and board we supply comes from sustainably managed forests. We continually review our environmental practices across warehousing, logistics and waste management to reduce our footprint.

Certifications & Standards

Scalderhurst is committed to responsible sourcing, environmental stewardship and maintaining the highest standards of quality across our operations.

FSC Certified

Our Forest Stewardship Council chain-of-custody certification guarantees that FSC-labelled products we supply can be traced back to responsibly managed forests. This gives our customers full confidence in the provenance and sustainability credentials of their materials.

PEFC Certified

We also hold Programme for the Endorsement of Forest Certification chain-of-custody status. PEFC certification provides an additional internationally recognised assurance that the wood fibre in our products originates from sustainably managed sources.

Quality Management

Rigorous quality controls are embedded at every stage of our operation. From incoming goods inspection and storage conditions through to conversion tolerances and dispatch checks, we maintain documented procedures that ensure consistency and traceability across all products.

Environmental Responsibility

Beyond certification, we actively work to minimise our environmental impact. Our warehousing operations prioritise energy efficiency, we optimise delivery routes to reduce emissions, and we manage waste streams responsibly with a focus on recycling and material recovery.

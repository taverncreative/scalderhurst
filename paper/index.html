Woodfree Coated

Premium wood-free coated papers with a smooth, printable surface for high-quality colour reproduction. Held in stock in a range of weights and finishes from leading global manufacturers, with in-house sheeting and slitting available to any size you need.

Applications for Woodfree Coated

  • Brochures
  • Corporate literature and annual reports
  • High-end leaflets and flyers
  • Point-of-sale material
  • Product catalogues
  • Premium direct-mail campaigns

Not stocking the exact grade you need? We source non-stock items through our global supply network — giving you access to hard-to-find specifications without sourcing direct from a mill.

Woodfree Uncoated

Uncoated wood-free papers with excellent print opacity, ink take-up and machinability. A core stock item across a broad weight range, supplied in sheets and reels to converters, envelope manufacturers and commercial printers.

Applications for Woodfree Uncoated

  • Envelope reels for envelope manufacturers
  • Letterheads and business stationery
  • Books, directories and educational print
  • Forms, pads and notebooks
  • Direct-mail and transactional printing

Woodfree uncoated is one of our most widely supplied grades into the UK envelope trade. Reel widths and diameters can be cut to specification on our in-house rewinders.

LWC (Lightweight Coated)

Lightweight coated papers engineered for long-run, four-colour publication printing. Good printability at lower substance means higher page counts per tonne — and lower postage costs on bulk mailings.

Applications for LWC

  • Magazine printing
  • Supplements and inserts
  • Catalogues and mail-order publications
  • High-volume promotional print
  • Newspaper supplements

Held in stock in core magazine grades with global sourcing available for specialist substrates.

Cut-Size Copier Paper

Ready-to-use cut-size office papers in A4, A3 and related formats — a reliable, in-stock business paper for day-to-day office use and volume printing.

Applications for Copier Paper

  • General office printing and photocopying
  • Business documents and correspondence
  • Forms and administrative print
  • Internal reports and presentations
  • High-volume print rooms

Supplied in cartons or pallet quantities. Bulk office requirements can be scheduled as a standing order.

Envelope Reels

Envelope grades supplied as reels, rewound to the exact width and diameter your envelope-making line requires. A long-standing speciality of ours — we understand how UK envelope manufacturers run and the tolerances their machines expect.

Applications for Envelope Reels

  • High-volume envelope manufacturing
  • White and manilla envelope stock
  • Window envelopes and specialist formats
  • Banker and pocket envelopes
  • Direct-mail envelope runs

Reels rewound to your specification in-house on our wide-format rewinder — reducing waste and keeping your production line running at full speed.
